# Product Spec: Norwegian Shadowing App

Norwegian learners need repeated listening and speaking practice with real audio, readable transcripts, and quick vocabulary help.

This app lets a learner:

- Listen to short Norwegian lessons.
- Read a synced transcript.
- Click a sentence to seek.
- Repeat the current segment.
- Toggle inline English glosses.
- Practice offline after static assets are cached.

The app remains local/static: raw lessons are processed ahead of time into `lesson.json`, `captions.vtt`, and `audio.mp3`.

Primary written standard: Bokmål. Metadata language code: `nb`. ASR language code: `no`.
